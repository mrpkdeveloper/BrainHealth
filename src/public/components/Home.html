Diagnosis

Most psychiatrists and psychologists use the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders(DSM-5) to diagnose mental health disorders. Picasso's Blue Period

In the latter part of 1901, Picasso sank into a severe depression and blue tones began to dominate his paintings. His psychological state worsened as 1901 continued. Picasso, normally an outgoing socializer, withdrew from his friends. Picasso's bout of depression was to last several years.
